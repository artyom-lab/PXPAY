Technology

PXPay platform is a core business layer on top of any existing store of value such as core banking, blockchains, payment processors, etc. PXPay can either mirror an existing ledger system to extend its business logic to accommodate a specific use case, or be completely decoupled from existing user accounts.

PXPay Core services include core business logic and integrations with blockchains (e.g. Stellar, Ethereum, Bitcoin etc.), off-chain stores of value, payment processors and more. The system is fully integrated with our Admin Suite and Mobile Wallet App.
